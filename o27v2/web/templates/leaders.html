Methodology. All rate stats are PA- or 27-outs-denominated to fit the O27 structure (one continuous 27-out half per side). PAVG = H/PA is the headline batting average; BAVG = H/AB is the secondary "stayer profile" metric and can exceed 1.000 thanks to multi-hit ABs via stays. ERA, WHIP, K/27, BB/27 are per 27 outs. FIP is recalibrated each render so league FIP = league ERA — a 4.50 FIP in a 12.00-ERA league is well above league average. OPS+ / wOBA+ / ERA+ are all relativized to live league baselines (100 = avg, higher = better — note ERA+ inverts since lower raw ERA is better). WAR uses an O27-fitted runs-per-win factor (~21 vs MLB's ~10) so the scale reads correctly. Win/loss credits an SP with ≥ 12 outs and the lead, otherwise the most-effective reliever.
