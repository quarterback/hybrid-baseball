Game #1353 surfaces super-inning rendering bugs. Six things:

1. SUPER-INNING ROUNDS NOT LABELED. Tae-Hoon Nakamura appears 3 
   times as separate rows. Beau Finn appears 3 times. No row 
   indicates which is regulation, which is SI Round 1, etc. 
   Either:
   (a) Group rows by section with headers: "Regulation", 
       "Super-Inning 1", "Super-Inning 2", "Super-Inning 3"
   (b) Add a column to each row indicating the phase
   (c) Aggregate per-player across all rounds with a single 
       totals row per player, and show round-by-round breakdown 
       only on click/expand

2. OS% IS BROKEN ONCE SUPER-INNINGS HAPPEN. Beau Finn's middle 
   row shows 104% OS%. Totals row shows 337%. Outs share is a 
   percentage of 27 only during regulation. In super-innings, 
   the denominator is the round's out cap (5 dismissals). 
   Either:
   (a) Compute OS% per phase (regulation OS% out of 27, SI 
       round OS% out of 5)
   (b) Use a unified denominator: total team outs across all 
       phases (regulation 27 + SI rounds) and compute OS% 
       against that
   
   Whichever, the displayed value can never exceed 100%. 
   A 337% value is mathematical nonsense.

3. PITCHER OUTS TOTAL 91. Three super-innings of 5-dismissal 
   rounds + 27 regulation = 42 max. 91 is impossible. Investigate:
   (a) Are super-innings actually stopping after a winner is 
       determined, or are they running indefinitely?
   (b) Is the "out" counter accumulating correctly across phases?
   (c) Are dismissals being counted as outs correctly?

4. STAY INDICATOR "S" SUFFIX IS BACK ON PLAYER NAMES. Tyler 
   Larsen S, Dawel Cedillo S. Spec was clear: player names 
   are clean, stays don't surface in name display. Fix.

5. "Team (legacy CS / FC / non-batter outs)" PATCH ROW IS BACK 
   in totals. Same fix as before — attribute outs correctly in 
   the engine, don't patch the totals row.

6. SUPER-INNING SHOULD BE A SEPARATE BOX SCORE SECTION, not 
   appended rows in the regulation batting/pitching tables. 
   Suggested layout:
   
   - Regulation Batting (12 rows per team)
   - Regulation Pitching (3-5 rows per team)
   - Super-Inning Round 1 (5 batters per team + pitchers used)
   - Super-Inning Round 2 (only if needed)
   - Super-Inning Round 3 (only if needed)
   - Game Totals (regulation + super-innings, one row per player)
   
   This is how cricket renders Super Overs and how MLB renders 
   extra-inning totals. Don't smash all phases into one table.

CONFIRM before next sim:
- Super-innings are stopping after a winner is determined per 
  spec (each side fields 5 batters, bats until 5 dismissals or 
  team is ahead at end of round, repeat until winner).
- Regulation outs cap at 27 per side and never accumulate further 
  in the regulation totals.
