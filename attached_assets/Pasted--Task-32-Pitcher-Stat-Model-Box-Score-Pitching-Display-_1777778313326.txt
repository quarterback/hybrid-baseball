# Task #32 — Pitcher Stat Model + Box Score Pitching Display + Hitter Stat Refinements

## What & Why

Replace the current pitcher stat line with one that fits O27's structure (drop "innings pitched," use BF, add OUT and OS%). Replace the play-by-play tab with a pitching log. On the hitter side, add **H/AB** (hits per at-bat) — the cleanest way to surface multi-hit at-bats as a regular baseball stat without ever naming the stay mechanic. Add **RPG** at the team level.

## Done looks like

### Per-game box score — pitching table

Columns, in this order: **BF · P · OS% · OUT · H · R · ER · BB · K · HR**

- **BF** — batters faced.
- **P** — pitches thrown.
- **OS%** — outs share, percentage of the team's 27 outs this pitcher recorded. Whole percent (e.g. `33%`), not decimal.
- **OUT** — out number when the pitcher's last batter's PA ended. For the final pitcher of a half, this is `27`. For a pitcher pulled mid-half, it's the team's out count when his last batter was retired or reached. If a pitching change occurs mid-PA, OUT is the team's out count at the moment of the change.
- **H, R, ER, BB, K, HR** — standard counting stats.

Header row labeled. Monospace numerics. Right-aligned numeric columns.

### Per-game box score — batting table

Add a new column: **H/AB**.

Final column set: **PA · AB · R · H · H/AB · 2B · 3B · HR · RBI · BB · K · OR · AVG**

- **H/AB** — hits divided by at-bats. Shown to three decimals (e.g. `1.250`). A batter who got two hits in one of his ABs via the stay mechanic will show H/AB above 1.000. The stay mechanic is invisible to the reader but its effect is visible in this column.
- **OR** — outs recorded by the batter (from the previous spec).
- AVG kept; H/AB is additive, not a replacement.

Drop STY column entirely.

### Pitching log replaces play-by-play tab

The play-by-play tab is removed from the game results page. In its place, a "Pitching" tab (or section) shows the pitching tables for both teams in the format above. No prose narrative output anywhere in the game UI.

The engine should stop emitting play-by-play strings to any output the UI consumes. Internal event tracking for stat aggregation stays — but strings like `Sean Norris STAYS at the plate / Hit credited (stay) / Stay — fresh 0-0 count` are no longer rendered, stored, or sent to the frontend.

### Per-pitcher season totals (player page)

Columns: **G · BF · P · OS% · ERA · WHIP · AOR · K · BB · HR · K/9 · BB/9**

- **G** — games (appearances).
- **AOR** — average out reached. Mean of OUT values across all appearances. Display as one decimal (e.g. `21.4`). Workhorses post AOR ~22, closers ~26, long relief ~14.
- **ERA** — earned runs per 27 outs faced. Same name, different denominator. League average lands near 11.50 in O27. Add "(O27 league avg: ~11.50)" footnote on the pitcher leaderboard page.
- **WHIP** — walks + hits per 27 outs faced.
- **K/9, BB/9** — keep names. Math is per 27 outs faced.

### Per-batter season totals (player page)

Add columns: **H/AB · OR**.

Column set: **G · PA · AB · R · H · H/AB · 2B · 3B · HR · RBI · BB · K · OR · AVG · OBP · SLG · OPS**

H/AB also appears as a sortable leaderboard column on the batting leaders page — players who use the stay mechanic productively will float to the top.

### Team-level totals (team page + standings)

Add: **RPG · RAPG**.

- **RPG** — runs per game scored.
- **RAPG** — runs per game allowed.
- Standings table includes both as columns alongside W, L, PCT, GB, L10, Streak.

### FIP recalibration

Re-derive the FIP constants against the O27 run environment. League-average FIP should equal league-average ERA (~11.50). Document the new constant in code so it can be retuned later.

## Out of scope

- Splits by 27-out arc (outs 1–9 / 10–18 / 19–27) — later task.
- Joker leverage index — later task.
- WAR / wOBA / wRC+ recalibration — later task.
- Multi-hit AB rate as its own column or leaderboard — H/AB already captures it.

## Steps

1. Add `pitches_thrown` counter to `o27/engine/state.py` (per-pitcher per-game). Increment on every pitch event.
2. Track `out_when_pulled` per pitcher per appearance. Set on pitching change or end of half.
3. Remove play-by-play emission. Engine still tracks events internally for stat aggregation; stop populating any `play_by_play` field in the game output JSON; stop rendering it anywhere.
4. Remove the play-by-play tab from `o27/web/templates/game.html` and stats site equivalents.
5. Update box score pitching table template with the new column set and header row.
6. Update box score batting table: add H/AB column, drop STY column entirely, keep OR column.
7. Compute AOR in the season aggregator. Add to player page season totals.
8. Add ERA, WHIP, K/9, BB/9 with O27 denominators (per 27 outs faced). Recalibrate FIP constant.
9. Add H/AB to player page season totals and to batting leaderboard as a sortable column.
10. Add RPG and RAPG to team page totals and standings table.
11. Add "(O27 league avg: ~11.50)" footnote on pitcher leaderboard page.
12. Verify `Yunel Florial exits (23 BF this spell)` style debug log lines are no longer emitted anywhere user-facing.
